Data Storage and Privacy

Important: We do not collect or store any personal information on our servers.

All game data (including your progress, settings, stars, purchased items, and achievements) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

You can delete all game data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the Application, or
  • Clearing app data from your device's Settings

Anonymous Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics, such as:

This information helps us improve the app's performance and user experience. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Advertising

The Application displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process information for personalized advertising purposes, including:

You can opt-out of personalized advertising in your device settings. For more information, see Google's privacy policy below.

Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services. Each service has its own privacy policy:

What We Do NOT Collect

The Application does not collect, store, or share:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your precise location or GPS data
  • Photos, files, or contacts from your device
  • Any personally identifiable information
  • Credit card or payment information (all purchases are handled by Google Play Store)

Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data to delete. However, if you believe a child has provided any information through third-party services, please contact us at mosugametr@gmail.com.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since all game data is stored locally on your device and we do not store any user data on our servers:

  • You have full control over your data
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local game data
  • There is no need to request data deletion from us, as we don't have your data

Anonymous analytics data collected by Firebase is retained according to Google's data retention policies.

Security

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Since we do not collect or store personal information on our servers, there is minimal risk to your data. All game data remains secure on your device.
